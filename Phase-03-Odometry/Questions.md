# Phase 03 — Questions

## Goal

Understand how robots estimate movement, why those estimates drift over time, and why external correction methods such as localization and SLAM are necessary.

## Core Questions

1. What is odometry?
2. How do wheel encoders work?
3. How does the robot estimate distance traveled?
4. What causes odometry drift?
5. Why is odometry never perfectly accurate?
6. Can odometry work without GPS?
7. Can odometry work without LiDAR?
8. Why does wheel slip matter?

## Additional Concepts

9. What is dead reckoning?
10. What is error accumulation?
11. What is systematic error?
12. What is random error?
13. What is calibration?
14. What is sensor fusion?
15. What is an Extended Kalman Filter, or EKF?
16. How are odometry, localization, and SLAM related?
17. What are the primary MentorPi coordinate frames used in this phase?

## Simpleton Test

18. Explain odometry to a 10-year-old.
